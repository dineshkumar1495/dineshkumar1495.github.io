Credit Risk Assessment for Loan Approval

Data-Driven Approaches to Financial Decision-Making

About the project

This report explores the application of data analytics in credit risk assessment for loan approval. The analysis uses Logistic Regression and compares its performance with KNN classifier. Two different datasets are used for this analysis and the key variables in the datasets are credit score, applicantâ€™s income, marital status and so on. The business decision is critical for financial institutions because accurate credit risk assessment helps in identifying good candidates from bad ones thus minimizing bad debts and increase profitability. Findings reveals Logistic Regression performs better as it is more suitable algorithm for this business question.

Key Highlights

  • Compared Logistic Regression vs. KNN for credit risk assessment
  • Analyzed financial datasets to improve loan approval decisions.
  • Demonstrated the impact of data preprocessing on model accuracy
