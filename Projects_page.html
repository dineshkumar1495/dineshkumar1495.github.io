Credit Risk Assessment for Loan Approval

Data-Driven Approaches to Financial Decision-Making

In this project, I explored how data analytics and machine learning can improve credit risk assessment for loan approvals. Using Logistic Regression and K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN), I analyzed financial data, including credit scores, applicant income, and loan history, to predict loan approval outcomes. The study found that Logistic Regression performed better due to its interpretability and efficiency in handling structured financial data.

Digital Marketing Analytics: Data-Driven Growth Strategies

This project analyzes digital marketing performance using Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to track traffic, engagement, and conversion rates for an e-commerce store. By examining organic search, paid ads, social media, and email marketing, I identified key trends that drive sales and user engagement. The insights led to strategic recommendations for conversion optimization, personalized marketing, and audience segmentation to boost revenue.

Data Security & Cyber Risk Analysis

A comprehensive security assessment of a financial services firm, identifying risks like data breaches, phishing attacks, and insider threats. It also evaluates security best practices and proposes improvements based on the CIA Triad.
